Welcome to the Support page!

We are here to assist you in navigating our website's recommendations feature. Our goal is to help you discover new and exciting content in the categories of Music, Books, Movies, and Games. Below, you will find some helpful information to guide you through the process:

  • collectionsCategories
    • Music: Explore a wide range of musical genres and discover new artists, albums, and songs.

    • Books: Dive into various literary genres, find captivating novels, non-fiction works, and more.

    • Movies: Immerse yourself in the world of cinema with movies from different genres, including action, drama, comedy, and more.

    • Games: Embark on thrilling gaming adventures with titles spanning different genres and platforms.

  • ads_clickSelecting a Category:

    Through the Homepage you get to the Recommendations page where you can click on the category that interests you the most. You will be redirected to the respective page dedicated to that category.

  • checklistChoosing a Genre:

    Once you're on the category page, choose a specific genre that aligns with your preferences. We have a diverse range of options available to cater to different tastes.

  • searchDiscovering Content:

    After selecting a genre, our system will generate a random object from that category. You will see its cover, name, and additional details to pique your interest.

  • syncExploring Further:

    If you're not satisfied with the current recommendation, you can always click again to generate another random object within the same genre.

We hope this guide helps you navigate our recommendations smoothly. Should you have any further questions or encounter any difficulties, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
Enjoy exploring and discovering new content on our website!
